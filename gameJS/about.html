About SnakeGo

Welcome to SnakeGo, the classic snake game brought to life with a modern twist! SnakeGo provides an exciting gaming experience that takes you back to the nostalgia of the original Snake game, while incorporating new features and challenges.

Key Features:

  • Responsive and intuitive controls for an immersive gameplay experience.
  • Customizable settings to tailor the game to your preferences.
  • Compete with friends and see who can achieve the highest score!

SnakeGo is not just a game; it's a journey into the world of retro gaming with a contemporary touch. Whether you're a long-time fan of Snake or a newcomer to the genre, SnakeGo promises endless hours of entertainment.

Game Rules:

  • If the snake touches the box border or collides with its own body, the game is over.
  • When the snake eats, it grows longer, increasing the challenge.

Leaderboard:

If you are logged in, your high scores will be recorded on the leaderboard. Compete with other players and strive for the top spot!

Note: If you are not logged in, your game data will not be saved on the leaderboard.
